About the Founder

Amina Gadzhayeva

Amina created this brand for women who want wellness and beauty to feel light, calm, and modern. Her vision combines clean formulas with a soft, minimal aesthetic—so that taking care of yourself never feels heavy or complicated.

“For me, beauty is a quiet moment—simple routines, thoughtful ingredients, and design that lets you breathe.”

— Amina Gadzhayeva

Brand philosophy

  • Light, natural colors and modern, readable typography.
  • Focused product range so every choice feels clear and intentional.
  • Founder-led quality, transparency, and a calm shopping experience.
